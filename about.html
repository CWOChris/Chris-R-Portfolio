About Me
I have been online since I was 7 years old. From an early age, I took a keen interest in computers and, more importantly, the internet. I have wanted to develop my own code from a young age but spent ten years in the Army after high school in pursuit of other passions. I decided in early 2023 that I was in need of a change in pace and lifestyle, and enrolled in the University of Minnesota's Full Stack (MERN) Web Development BootCamp by EdX. I am currently enrolled in the BootCamp and anticipate completion at the end of February, 2024. I'm excited for this next chapter in life and often find myself losing track of time while coding the BootCamp projects.
